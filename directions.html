Chicken Burrito

Step-by-Step directions to make your burrito.

  1. Grill or bake chicken breast in a Pyrex dish at 350 degrees for approximately 20 minutes.
  2. Boil rice
  3. Cool chicken and slice thinly.
  4. Place tortilla in microwave between 2 paper towels and cook on high for 10 seconds.
  5. Put chicken, rice, cheese, salsa, sour cream and any other ingredients you choose into tortilla and roll up.
